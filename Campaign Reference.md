# The Sigmarite Uprising — Campaign Reference

The authoritative record of the campaign: rules, roster, session log, and reward catalogue. Battle write-ups live in `scenarios/`; this document carries the summaries and standings.

---

## The Premise

A slave uprising. The Chaos Dwarfs (Dawi-Zharr) hold the forges and the slaves; the Empire Sigmarites rise against them. The rebels begin poorly equipped and must seize better arms and magic as the war progresses.

---

## Campaign Rules

### List Building
- **Heroes only** — warbands are built entirely from Hero-level characters; no core or rank-and-file units.
- **350 points** per warband.
- Up to **15% rare**.
- **One magic item** per warband, worth a maximum of **25 points**.

### Death
When a character is eliminated during a battle, roll a D6 at the end of the battle. On a **1** the character is dead and removed from the campaign. On a 2+ they survive.

### Ruleset
Warhammer Armies Project (WAP), **v3.1** *(exact version to be confirmed)*.

## The Warbands

Five commanders. The war turns on two: **Anders**, who leads the Empire slaves in revolt, and **Frederik**, whose Chaos Dwarfs hold the whip. The other three fly no fixed banner — they march with whatever host the moment demands, allies and opportunists both.

| Player | Army | Role |
|--------|------|------|
| Anders | The Empire | The Uprising — the Sigmarite rebels |
| Frederik | Chaos Dwarfs | The Slavers — the Dawi-Zharr masters |
| Kristian | Tomb Kings | Recurring |
| Jesper | Orcs & Goblins · Ogre Kingdoms · Warriors of Chaos · Dwarfs | Recurring — no fixed army |
| Daniel | Lizardmen *(also High Elves, Daemons of Chaos)* | Recurring |
