**Role:** Recurring — no permanent banner.

Jesper fields whichever host the battle demands:

- **Orcs & Goblins**
- **Ogre Kingdoms**
- **Warriors of Chaos**

*Each warband is built to 350 points, Heroes only. The army — and its heroes — are recorded per battle as they take the field.*
